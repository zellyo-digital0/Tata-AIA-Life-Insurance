Improve your Health

Participate and track your physical activities by linking your fitness device/app to the Tata AIA Vitality App. It motivates you to achieve your fitness goals through challenges and you earn points along the way.

Earn Upto 50 Points Daily

  • 30mins of physical activity at >= 60% heart rate*

  • 7,500 - 9,999 Steps

Earn Upto 100 Points Daily

  • 30mins of physical activity at >= 70% heart rate*

  • 10,000+ Steps

*Age-related maximum heart rate = 220 – Current Age

For 10,000+ Steps the corresponding Heart Rate challenge shall be either 30 minutes of physical activity at >=70% of age-related maximum heart rate or 60 minutes of physical activity at >=60% of age-related maximum heart rate*

Rewards For a better you

Opting for TATA AIA Vitality provides you an upfront discount on your first-year premium. Start at bronze status with zero Vitality points. Earn points through the various activities in the ‘Know your health’ and ‘Improve your health’ sections and move up the ladder to Platinum status and enjoy renewal rewards. Renewal rewards may be in the form of Discount on Premium, Premium Cashback, Cover Booster or Annual health cashback. Max upfront discount can be up to 10% and max renewal discount can be up to 30% depending on plan opted.
